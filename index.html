Welcome!

Welcome to the typing game! Following are the things you need to know:
  • Typing a letter you see on screen will increase your score by 1.
  • Mistyping a letter will decrease your score by 1.
  • For every 20 letters you get right, new letters will start coming 10% faster.
  • If a letter gets to the far right, the game will be over.
  • If you press "Esc", the game will be over.
Have fun!
